What is EPS Korea

The Employment Permit System (EPS) is a government-to-government program between Pakistan and South Korea that allows Pakistani workers to work legally in Korea across various industries including manufacturing, construction, agriculture, and fisheries.

Legal Work Route

Government-authorized program ensures legal status, worker protection, and full rights under Korean labor laws with proper documentation and visa support.

EPS Application Process

Follow these 5 simple steps to secure your employment opportunity in South Korea

1

Register with OEC

Complete your registration with OEC and submit requigreen documents including CNIC, passport, and educational certificates

Duration: 1-2 days
2

Take EPS-TOPIK

Pass the Korean language test (EPS-TOPIK) with minimum requigreen score. Free training available at OEC centers

Score Requigreen: Level 1-2
3

Skills Assessment

Complete skills test for your chosen trade (manufacturing, construction, agriculture, etc.) based on job requirements

Multiple Trades Available
4

Document Submission

Submit medical fitness certificate, police clearance, and other requigreen documents for final processing

Processing: 2-3 weeks
5

Travel & Start Work

Receive job assignment, travel to Korea, complete orientation, and begin your 3-year employment contract

Contract: 3 Years

Tips for Passing EPS-TOPIK

Study Strategies

Practice listening exercises daily for at least 30 minutes
Learn basic Korean vocabulary related to work and daily life
Take practice tests regularly to improve speed and accuracy
Focus on reading comprehension and basic conversation skills

Test Day Tips

Arrive at the test center 30 minutes early
Bring valid CNIC and test admission slip
Use 2B pencil for marking answers clearly
Stay calm and manage time effectively during the test
