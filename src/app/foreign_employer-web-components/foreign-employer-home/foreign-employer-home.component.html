Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about hiring through OEC Pakistan

What are the requirements to register as an employer?

You need valid business registration documents, a demand letter specifying job requirements, and company authorization letter. Our team will guide you through the complete documentation process during registration.

How long does the hiring process typically take?

The complete process from registration to worker deployment typically takes 30-45 days. This includes 3-5 days for approval, 7-14 days for candidate shortlisting, and 15-20 days for final processing and deployment.

What support do you provide during the hiring process?

We provide comprehensive support including candidate pre-screening, interview coordination, translation services, document processing, visa assistance, and pre-departure training coordination. Our dedicated account managers ensure smooth communication throughout.

Are there any costs associated with registration?

Registration is completely free. We charge transparent government fees only for successful placements. All costs are clearly outlined upfront with no hidden charges. Payment is required only after successful worker deployment.

What industries do you serve?

We serve all major industries including construction, manufacturing, healthcare, hospitality, agriculture, domestic services, and technical trades. Our database includes workers with diverse skill sets and experience levels.
