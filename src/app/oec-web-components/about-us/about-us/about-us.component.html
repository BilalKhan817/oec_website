ABOUT US

Overseas Employment Corporation (OEC)

Established 1976 | Ministry of Overseas Pakistanis & Human Resource Development

Introduction

The Overseas Employment Corporation (OEC) is the only government-owned public sector organization in Pakistan responsible for the recruitment and deployment of Pakistani manpower to reputable foreign employers.
Founded in 1976, OEC operates under the Ministry of Overseas Pakistanis & Human Resource Development (OP&HRD) and adheres to the Emigration Ordinance, 1979. It serves as a reliable bridge between Pakistani job seekers and foreign employers, ensuring ethical recruitment, legal migration, and welfare of the workforce abroad.

Our Legal Mandate

  • Registered under: The Companies Act, 1913 (now the Companies Act, 2017)
  • The Emigration Ordinance, 1979
  • Governed in coordination with the Bureau of Emigration & Overseas Employment (BEOE) and other regulatory authorities

Our Role

  • Transparent, merit-based, and cost-effective overseas employment
  • Employer-led recruitment aligned with skill demand
  • Documentation and post-selection support services
  • No private middlemen or agents involved
  • Secure pre-departure and post-deployment processes

Our Target Sectors

  • 🏥 Healthcare (Nurses, Doctors, Paramedics)
  • 🏗️ Skilled Labor (Construction, Mechanical, Electrical)
  • 🧑‍🏭 Manufacturing & Industrial Sectors
  • 🧑‍💼 Administrative & Technical Services
  • 🇰🇷 Special Programs: Korea EPS (Employment Permit System)

Our Unique Strength

  • ✅ Sole public sector manpower exporter
  • ✅ Official recruitment partner for foreign governments
  • ✅ Data-driven system for job-matching
  • ✅ 100% digital pre-registration and shortlisting
  • ✅ Transparent fee structure with full public disclosure

Our Vision

To be Pakistan’s most trusted gateway for safe and dignified overseas employment.

Our Mission

To facilitate legal and transparent employment of Pakistani workers in global labor markets by adhering to high standards of integrity, efficiency, and public service.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity – Ethical practices in all operations
  • Transparency – No hidden processes or fees
  • Merit – Fair and equal opportunities for all
  • Efficiency – Swift, accurate processing
  • Accountability – Public trust and auditability
  • Dignity – Respect for every applicant’s journey

Our Impact

  • 25,000+ trained in Korean & Japanese languages
  • Government-to-Government (G2G) agreements with KSA, Korea, Japan, Italy, and more
