Strategic Governance & Oversight of OEC

Introduction

The Board of Directors (BoD) of the Overseas Employment Corporation (OEC) provides strategic leadership, policy direction, and corporate governance in accordance with public sector regulations. It ensures that OEC's operations remain transparent, accountable, and aligned with national interests and international standards.

Composition of the Board

The Board comprises experienced professionals, government officials, and industry experts, nominated by the Ministry of Overseas Pakistanis & Human Resource Development (OP&HRD) and other relevant authorities.

Responsibilities of the Board

  • Approving OEC's strategic plans and policies
  • Reviewing annual reports, audits, and budgets
  • Overseeing high-value procurement and recruitment programs
  • Ensuring alignment with national migration and employment strategies
  • Monitoring risk management, ethics, and legal compliance
