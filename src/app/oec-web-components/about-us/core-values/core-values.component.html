OUR MISSION, VISION & CORE VALUES

🎯 Our Mission

To facilitate secure, fair, and transparent overseas employment opportunities for Pakistani citizens by connecting them with reputable foreign employers through government-to-government and verified private channels.

We are committed to empowering individuals, strengthening families, and contributing to Pakistan's economic growth through the ethical export of skilled and unskilled manpower.

🔮 Our Vision

To be Pakistan's most trusted, innovative, and responsive public sector institution for international employment services — recognized globally for our integrity, efficiency, and people-first approach.

We envision a future where every Pakistani has access to dignified overseas employment through legal, efficient, and technology-enabled processes.

💎 Our Core Values

🔒

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty and fairness in all our dealings with applicants, employers, and partners.

🔍

Transparency

Our recruitment, fee structure, and operations are fully public, traceable, and free of middlemen.

⚖️

Merit

We ensure fair competition, equal opportunity, and merit-based shortlisting for all applicants.

⭐

Excellence

We strive to continuously improve service quality, stakeholder satisfaction, and institutional performance.

📋

Accountability

We are responsible to the people of Pakistan and committed to timely, auditable service delivery.

👷

Dignity of Work

We respect every occupation and ensure workers are treated with honor and fairness.

🚀

Innovation

We embrace technology to modernize recruitment, increase reach, and simplify access to opportunities.
