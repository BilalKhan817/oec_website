International Agreements for Ethical and Government-to-Government Recruitment

What are MoUs?

A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) is a formal agreement signed between the Government of Pakistan (via OEC) and foreign governments, institutions, or companies to facilitate safe, transparent, and regulated overseas employment of Pakistani workers.

These MoUs lay the legal foundation for:

  • Verified foreign job demands
  • Protection of workers' rights
  • Transparent recruitment mechanisms
  • Government-to-Government collaboration
  • Training and skill development partnerships
