Transparent, Government-Approved Charges Only

Our Commitment to Transparency

OEC operates on a no-profit, no-loss basis. All fees collected from job seekers are officially notified, deposited through government challans, and used to cover actual processing costs only.

We do not work with agents or allow any unauthorized payments. All charges are clearly displayed at the time of registration and documented in your application portal.
