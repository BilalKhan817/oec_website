Mandatory Training Before Traveling Abroad for Work

Why Orientation Matters

Pre-departure orientation is a mandatory educational session organized by OEC to prepare selected workers for life, work, and legal compliance in the destination country. It is designed to reduce exploitation, increase cultural awareness, and ensure workers fully understand their rights and responsibilities.
