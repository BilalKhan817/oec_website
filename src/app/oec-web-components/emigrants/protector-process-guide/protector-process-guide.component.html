Mandatory Emigration Clearance for Working Abroad

What is a Protector?

A Protector of Emigrants (POE) stamp is a legal clearance issued by the Government of Pakistan through the Protectorate of Emigrants. It is mandatory for all Pakistani citizens traveling abroad for employment.

⚠️ Important: Without protector clearance, a worker is not legally recognized as an emigrant, and may face legal consequences, travel denial, or lack of support abroad.

Who Needs It?

  • ✅ All selected candidates under OEC-managed overseas recruitment
  • ✅ Workers traveling to Gulf countries, Korea, Italy, Malaysia, etc.
  • ❌ Not required for visitors, students, or tourists

Step-by-Step Process

1

Receive job offer/selection confirmation via OEC

2

Visit nearest Protectorate of Emigrants Office with documents

3

Submit application and required fees via bank challan

4

Complete biometric registration

5

Collect stamped passport and protector receipt

Required Documents

  • 📄 Valid CNIC
  • 📄 Valid Passport
  • 📄 Original & attested job contract
  • 📄 Medical fitness certificate
  • 📄 Insurance documents
  • 📄 4 passport-sized photographs
  • 📄 OEC Letter/Selection Confirmation
  • 📄 Bank deposit receipt (protector fee)
