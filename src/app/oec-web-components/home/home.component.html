About OEC

Building Careers, Connecting Nations Since 1976

The Overseas Employment Corporation (OEC) was founded in 1976 as a Public Sector recruitment agency. It is registered as a private limited company under the Companies Act of 1913 (now 2017, Section-42).

OEC operates under the administrative control of the Ministry of Overseas Pakistanis and Human Resource Development. Governed by SECP's Corporate Governance Rules (2013), OEC connects skilled Pakistani workers to global opportunities.
