Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to register?

Your valid BEOE license, business registration, and company authorization letter.

How long does approval take?

Typically 24-48 hours once we verify your BEOE credentials.

Is there a registration fee?

No, registration is completely free for licensed OEPs.

Can I manage multiple BEOE accounts?

Yes, you can link multiple BEOE licenses to one OEC account.
